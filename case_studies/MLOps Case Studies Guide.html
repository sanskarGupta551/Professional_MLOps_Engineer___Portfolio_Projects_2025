Learning Objectives by Study Focus
Infrastructure & Architecture
Understand how to design and implement scalable ML infrastructure that grows with business needs.
Skills: Platform design, scaling strategies, multi-cloud deployment, cost optimization
Production Deployment
Learn deployment strategies, serving patterns, and operational practices for reliable ML systems.
Skills: Blue-green deployment, A/B testing, canary releases, monitoring, alerting
Data & Feature Engineering
Master data pipeline design, feature engineering, and serving infrastructure for ML systems.
Skills: Feature stores, data validation, streaming pipelines, feature serving
Governance & Compliance
Understand regulatory requirements, bias detection, and governance frameworks for ML systems.
Skills: Model governance, bias detection, audit trails, regulatory compliance
Team Collaboration
Learn how to build effective MLOps teams and processes that scale across organizations.
Skills: Cross-functional collaboration, workflow design, tool selection, team processes
Business Impact
Understand how to measure and maximize the business value of ML investments and initiatives.
Skills: ROI measurement, business metrics, stakeholder management, value demonstration
